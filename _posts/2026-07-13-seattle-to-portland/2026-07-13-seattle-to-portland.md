


# 2026 Seattle to Portland

(draft)

## Training

1. Longest training ride was 64mi, did many times.
1. In retrospect, needed more gut and saddle training.

## Ride

### Timing

1. Felt a bit rushed, should have left earlier

### Body

1. Moderate leg cramping at 40mi on leg extension. Eased with stretching.
1. Cardio OK
1. No BONKing or tunnel vision
1. Power not great (slowest in group)
1. Severe elbow fatigue & pain
1. Moderate right foot pain at 180mi+
1. Ibuprofen at 110mi helpful.

### Nutrition

1. Ate a bit over half of planned food.
1. Skratch labs only for hours made stomach upset. Needed pairing with plain water.
1. Wanted more electrolytes
1. Wanted post-ride recovery meal of some kind
1. So much sugary food made gut unhappy, pepto was helpful

### Equipment

1. Wahoo with no navigating was 83% after 105mi. With navigating was 34% after about 50mi. TODO CS. Glad to have backup battery.
1. Radar very useful in some ways but distracting in others with so many bikes or cars.
1. Did not need 2 GoPro batteries
1. Wanted more USBC cables or chargers overnight. Needed to swap equipment out.

### Bike

1. Something wrong with handlebar fit (elbow pain)
1. Leg and knee fit OK
1. Saddle was painful but tolerable, likely just lack of training

### Pit Stops

1. Long waits for honey pots, needed more IMO
1. Not enough food (day 1 lunch stop)
1. At least one stop had no free water
1. In a group we could have pipelined some things

### Ettiquite and Behavior

1. Many more anti-cyclists than expected. Angry honks and punish passes. One pickup truck driver rolled coal over and over into the peloton. Saw on FB that this caused a crash.
1. Dangerous behavior from stopped cyclists, stopping on the road.

## Logistics

### Bags

1. Night before drop
1. Start line drop (Centralia full)
1. Centralia pick-up security (none, bags just left on ground)
1. Portland pick-up (easy & convenient)

### Lodging

#### Centralia Furnished Apartment

1. Pre-event communication could have been better. CS message went unanswered.

1. Not very clean, floors dirty
1. No pillows was unexpected given advertised as furnished with linens

#### Centralia Food

1. Incorrect signs and verbal guidance about vegan food. Would have preferred a menu posted online for each meal beforehand to plan.
1. In retrospect, I should have brought my own day 1 dinner

### Transportation

1. Easy, convenient, fast
1. Three different times posted in different places (715, 730, 630). Two paper signs, online
